BIO

"What never begins, Never ends"

Barcelona based DJ & producer, The Unborn Child. Beginning his interest in music at an early age, he studied electronic music production at the most prestigious academy in Buenos Aires.
Its name is a paradox that indicates a break between the past and the future, about what happened and what is about to happen.
Taking influence from from artists like Setaoc Mass, Kaiser, Lars Huismann, Tensal, VSK among others, his potential is realised via his intense studio output and live performances.
His definition of Techno is a fusion between aggressive percussive elements and organic atmospheres, creating expansive spaces that create a well refined and unique sound. Currently, his music is part of record labels such as Illegal Alien Records, Animal Farm, Otomo Trax, Trau-ma , ANAØH , Vector Functions, Terrafuria, Induxtriall Records and continues its expansion towards new horizons.
